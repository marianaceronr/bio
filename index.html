I'm a lawyer and product analyst with a rare mix of legal training, data science, and hands-on experience building products in high-stakes industries.

Then I crossed into tech, working in product and operations at Farmu, where I built Saludia from scratch: a new business unit, a team of 12, and a reverse-auction marketplace for pharmaceutical clients.

Along the way I picked up Python, SQL, Bubble.io, Power BI - and a deep obsession with how systems actually work, and how to make them work better for the people inside them.
